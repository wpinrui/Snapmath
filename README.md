# SnapMath

A camera-based math assistant that helps you solve, verify, and convert handwritten mathematical problems.

## Features

### Solve Mode
Point your camera at a handwritten math problem and watch it get solved step-by-step in real-time.

### Check Mode
Snap a photo of both a problem and your solution. The app verifies your work with visual feedback:
- **Green bounding boxes** — Correct steps/answers
- **Red bounding boxes** — Errors that need attention

### Test Mode
1. Enter your learning outcomes or topics
2. Receive generated practice questions
3. Solve the questions on paper
4. Snap and check your answers

### Convert Mode
Capture handwritten math and export it as:
- **LaTeX** — For academic papers and technical documents
- **MS Word equations** — For easy insertion into Word documents
